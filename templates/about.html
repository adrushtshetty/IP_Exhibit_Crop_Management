Crop Predicition


Since the beginning, increased crop production has been the ultimate goal of the farmer, and for this he has been constantly changing and finding new ways to produce and maximize the yield of the crops he has sown.


Different crops require different kinds of soil, different levels of nutrients and the right amount of water. The quantity of water required by the plant also depends on the growing season and the climate in which it is grown. By selecting the right crop for the given soil conditions and climate, the farmer can optimise yields, save on water required for irrigation and conserve the soil. Once adapted it becomes a sustainable tool to protect the soil from erosion.


The aim of this project was to help farmers grow the best crop with the soil at their disposal by entering the attributes of the soil like the level of N (Nitrogen),P (Phosphorus) & K (Potassium) ,pH level and the general humidity, temperature & rainfall of the region. After entering these values, the farmer can see the best crop he can cultivate in his soil through a machine learning algorithm.
